~FAQ~

    What classes do you offer?

    We offer a range of classes, including:


    Arial yoga
    Pilates
    Calisthenics

    Do I need prior experience to join?

    Not at all! We welcome all skill levels. Our beginner-friendly classes ensure that even if you've never stepped on a mat before, you'll feel comfortable and supported.



    What should I bring to class?

    Wear comfortable, breathable clothing that allows for movement. We provide mats and equipment, but feel free to bring your own if you prefer. For Pilates and calisthenics, grip socks are recommended.



    Can I try a class before committing to a membership?

    Yes! We offer a free trial class for first-time visitors. This way, you can experience the studio atmosphere and find the best fit for you.



    Do you offer private sessions?

    Yes, we provide one-on-one coaching tailored to your fitness goals. You can book a private session with any of our instructors.



    Is there a membership option?

    We offer flexible options, including drop-in classes, class packages, and monthly memberships. Check our pricing page for details.



    Do you have classes for prenatal or postnatal women?

    Absolutely! We offer specialized prenatal and postnatal yoga & Pilates classes to support mothers through every stage.



    Where is your studio located?

    We are located at:Hendrik Potgieter Rd, Christiaan de Wet Rd, Strubens Valley, Roodepoort, 1735



    How do I sign up for a class?

    You can book a class through our website's class schedule section. Simply choose a class, register, and show up ready to move!



    What if I need to cancel a class?

    We understand that life happens! You can cancel up to 12 hours before class without penalty. Late cancellations may be subject to a small fee.
