Our Services

At Derma Studios, we offer a range of classes designed to help women build strength, flexibility, and confidence in a supportive and empowering environment. Whether you're looking to unwind with yoga, strengthen your core with Pilates, or enhance your mobility through calisthenics, our expert-led sessions cater to all fitness levels.

Our Key Services:

    Aerial Yoga - Experience yoga from a whole new perspective using silk hammocks to improve flexibility, build core strength, and promote relaxation.
    Pilates - Strengthen and tone your body with low-impact exercises that enhance posture, stability, and core endurance.
    Calisthenics - Master bodyweight movements that improve strength, mobility, and coordination, helping you build a lean and toned physique.
